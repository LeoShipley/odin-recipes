Breaded Fingers of Haddock served with Fried Pommes and Tomatoe Puree

Ingredients:

  • Frozen Fish Fingers from any local supermarket
  • Grean Peas
  • Russet Potatoes
  • Vinegar (Optional)
  1. Preheat the oven and prepare the Haddock for the oven by adding a pinch of salt. Once the oven is ready cook the fingers for the required amount of time
  2. Peel and cut the potatos as desired and then prepare a heavy bottomed pot with 2 inches of oil
  3. Fry the potatos twice before allowing them to cool before serving
  4. Add the peas and 8ml of water person serving to a bowl, add a salt and pepper
  5. Cover the bowl in clingfilm and microwave the peas for 2-4 mins
  6. Drizzle vinegar over the chips and haddock for a more authentic taste (Optional)
