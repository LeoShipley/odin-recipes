Oven-roasted Beef served with Seasonal Vegetables and Yorkshire Pudding

Ingredients:

  • Beef Rump Joint
  • Onion
  • Potatoes
  • Season Specific Vegetables
  • Yorkshire Puddings
  1. Preheat the oven to 240
  2. Rub seasonsings of your choice over the beef and add into roasting dish
  3. Cut and prepare seasonal vegetables and potatos and add around the sides of the beef in the roasting tray
  4. Reduce the heat to 190 and put the roasting dish into the oven
  5. Remove roasting tray and leave vegetables and beef to cool, heat up yorkshire puddings in the microwave 5 mins before serving
