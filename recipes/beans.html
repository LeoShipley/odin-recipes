Baked Beans infused into Sourdough Toast

Ingredients:

  • Loaf of Sourdough
  • Tin of Baked Beans
  • Mature Cheddar
  1. Cut two slices of toast and toast gently until golden brown.
  2. Warm up baken beans on a low heat for 3-4 minutes
  3. Pour a generous amount of baken beans over the toast
  4. Grate the cheddar over the beans
